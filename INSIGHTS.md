# E-commerce Sales Insights

This document summarizes the key insights derived from the sales performance analysis.  
Each insight is aligned with a business question and followed by a clear takeaway.

---

## Revenue Performance

### Monthly Revenue Trend
Revenue remains relatively stable across the year, with a noticeable peak in mid-year and a decline toward year-end. This suggests seasonal demand patterns that can inform promotional planning and inventory management.

### Cumulative Revenue Growth
Cumulative revenue increases steadily month over month, indicating consistent sales performance without extreme volatility.

---

## Product & Category Insights

### Top Products by Revenue
A small group of products contributes a significant share of total revenue, indicating revenue concentration risk and the importance of maintaining availability for these products.

### Category Performance
Each category is dominated by a few high-performing products, suggesting opportunities for bundling or cross-selling lower-performing items.

---

## Platform & Geographic Insights

### Platform Contribution
Revenue is relatively balanced across platforms, though platform dominance varies by city, highlighting the need for localized platform strategies.

### City-Level Performance
A few cities account for a large share of total revenue, indicating strong regional demand and potential focus areas for marketing and logistics optimization.

---

## Customer Experience Insights

### High Revenue, Low Ratings
Several high-revenue products have below-average customer ratings, indicating potential quality or customer experience risks that could impact long-term performance.

### Ratings vs Revenue
Higher revenue does not always correlate with higher customer ratings, suggesting that sales volume alone does not reflect customer satisfaction.

---

## Trend Stability

### Rolling Revenue Trends
Rolling 3-month revenue trends show consistent performance, smoothing short-term fluctuations and providing a clearer view of underlying business stability.

---

## Overall Takeaway
The business demonstrates stable revenue performance driven by a small set of key products and regions. While growth opportunities exist, addressing customer experience risks and leveraging seasonality can improve long-term outcomes.
